## Support & Bug Reports

Found a bug or need help integrating the library?

- **GitHub Issues:** [https://github.com/Angular-Material-Dev/community/issues](https://https://github.com/Angular-Material-Dev/community/issues)

Not sure whether your use case requires a commercial license?

- **Email:** [support@angular-material.dev](mailto:support@angular-material.dev)
