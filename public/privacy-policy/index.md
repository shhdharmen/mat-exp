_Last updated: July 2026_

Angular Material Dev ("we", "us"), operated by **Nexcellentia**, runs the website at `expressive.angular-material.dev`. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Analytics

We use privacy-respecting, cookie-free analytics to understand how the documentation site is used — page views and navigation patterns only. No personally identifiable information is collected.

### Purchase Data

When you purchase a commercial license through our [Polar.sh store](https://polar.sh/ngm-dev/mat-expressive), your payment and account data is handled by Polar.sh. We receive only the information necessary to fulfill your license (email address and purchase confirmation). Polar.sh's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

### Contact & Support

When you email us at [support@angular-material.dev](mailto:support@angular-material.dev), we retain your email and message to respond to your inquiry. We do not share this with third parties.

## Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Session cookies may be set by the hosting infrastructure for technical purposes only.

## Third-Party Services

- **Vercel** — hosts this website. See [Vercel's privacy policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).
- **Polar.sh** — processes commercial license payments. See [Polar.sh's privacy policy](https://polar.sh/legal/privacy).
- **GitHub** — source code and issue tracking are hosted on GitHub. See [GitHub's privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you by emailing [support@angular-material.dev](mailto:support@angular-material.dev).
