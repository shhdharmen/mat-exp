## 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you purchase a commercial license and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within **30 days** of purchase — no questions asked.

To request a refund, email [support@angular-material.dev](mailto:support@angular-material.dev) with your order confirmation. Refunds are processed through Polar.sh and typically appear within 5–10 business days depending on your payment method.

## After 30 Days

Refund requests submitted more than 30 days after purchase are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We generally do not issue refunds after this period, but we will consider exceptional circumstances.

## Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued if:

- The license was terminated due to a violation of the [Terms and Conditions](/terms-and-conditions) or the license agreement.
- The refund request is determined to be in bad faith (e.g., purchasing, copying the source, then requesting a refund).

## License Termination Upon Refund

Upon issuing a refund, your commercial license is immediately revoked. You must cease all commercial use of `@ngm-dev/mat-expressive` and remove it from any commercial products.
